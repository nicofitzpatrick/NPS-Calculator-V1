NPS (Net Publisher Share) is the commission a music publisher claims on a particular licensing job.

This application takes in the full amount paid for the job, along with the client's (i.e. writer or sub-publisher) sync rate, and calculates the NPS.

This is the first version, designed only to handle works with a single client. Future versions will cater for multiple clients and more complex calculations.
